Problem


There are to many places to eat, people don't know where to actually go


For someone who lives in New York City I have numerous options for finding a place to eat. I love finding new places to eat but finding a good place to eat for an affordable price. I want an easier way to find good places to eat and be confident that I would enjoy it




Solution


Personal perspectives resonate with people

A friends opinion matters much more than you think

  • Find lists of spots your friends have gone to
  • Look at recommendations they've made
  • See if your friends have gone to a place your planning on going to

Add friends and start planning outings

  • Check if your friends want to go a restraunt together
  • Send them an invitation
  • Wait for them to accept or give you an excuse

Search up your friends or places to go to

  • Find trending posts of your query
  • Find friends who have gone that place
  • Find reviews of a spots you've been wanting to try





Ideation and Finding a Solution


People want a reliable source to find good places to eat.


Using survey monkey I was able to contact a variety of people to see what they would want in a food app. I was able to get 35 responses from people who live in New York City, and other Metropolitan areas . I was able to get a variety of people in the 16-25 age range and ask them where they find restaurants. I have compiled a few quotes I found interesting.

  • “I get alot of my food recommendations from tikok”

Reflection

I learned alot and also would do things a few things differently


I learned alot about the process of creating a product and the importance of research and testing. As one of my more in depth UX and product projects I enjoyed the experience and learned alot about the process of creating a product. But here are a few things I would have done differently if I had more time and done this again :


  1. Focus on the product While working on the project I had so many ideas that I wanted to incorpporate into the project. But I really wanted to one stick to my deadline of doing this in a month, while also sticking to the original motive of the product which was finding unique food expereinces with friends. But some of the other ideas that this project can evolve to are as follows:
    • Create lists for work spaces, cafes, or other kinds of expieriences in the city.
    • Allowing for messages to plan out a date and creating a calendar for outings throughout the week
    • A group outing function to invite more than one friend to a restraunt
  2. Do as many more iterations as I could. I iterated many times throughout the time I worked on this project and I learned to prioritize certain aspect to accomplish the goal of the product. If I went through more iterations I would have worked through other solutions as well and find more innovative solutions to the problems I was trying to solve. But each iteration was a learning expereince of its own.
  3. Be creative with your vision. Because this was a self driven projects I was able to work around with just the users goals in mind and by having this in mind I was able to create a product that was more unique and innovative. Although this was the case I based alot of this product on aspects of other products that I liked such as Tiktok and Instagram, but I believe I was able to enhance those products to fit the needs of what my product was envisioned to accomplish.
